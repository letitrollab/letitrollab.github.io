Description

The snowboard game is a realistic skill based snowboard game with natural physics set in multiple immersive mountain locations and snow parks.

Make epic stunts in the backcountry or just hang around in the park.

Create your own snowboard career in story mode where you meet other enthusiasts and pro boarders that will challenge you to learn new tricks or just get down the mountain.

Community

Hang out with a welcoming community. Get trick tips, help with challenges, share videos or just say hi. Say hello on Discord
